Primitive Cubic Structure Lattice structure

In this arrangement, the constituent lattice points hold the corner positions only.
In the first cell of the cubic unit, atoms are found only in the corners. Every atom in a corner is shared between cells in eight adjacent units.
There are four unit cells in the same layer as 4 in the upper (or lower) layer. Thus, a single unit of cell has only 1 / 8th of an atom.
